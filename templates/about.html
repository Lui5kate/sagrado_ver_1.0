Our Story

Sacred flavour, born from fire, family and the streets of Mexico.

Sagrado Cantina began as a dream thousands of kilometres away — a way to carry the flavour of my home in Chalco, México, into the heart of Auckland.

I grew up surrounded by the smells of charcoal, slow-simmered salsas, and tortillas hot off the comal. But most of all, I grew up tasting the food of my mother, Leticia — a woman with the kind of sazón you can’t learn in any school. Her cooking was bold, soulful, and always made with care. She learned from my grandmother, a strong woman from Tepito, and continued that legacy in our small kitchen — turning simple ingredients into something sacred.

After leaving Mexico at 24, I cooked my way through Australia and New Zealand — learning, growing, burning, starting over — until I was ready to bring something real to this side of the world.

Sagrado is the result of that journey. It's more than just a taco spot. It's a tribute to my mother's fire, my grandmother's strength, and the streets that raised me. It's a cantina for anyone who craves honest food, loud flavours, and a little bit of chaos — the good kind.

Everything we serve is made from scratch, just like mamá would — bold, balanced, and blessed.
